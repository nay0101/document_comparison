      39. 
      The Securities Trading Bonus Interest shall be calculated on a tiered basis based on the total amount of 
      trades by the Accountholder in a calendar month (“Total Monthly Traded Amount” or “MTA”).

      The MTA is derived as below:

      (i) 
      For Purchase transactions: 
      MTA = [Unit x Purchase Price] + Transaction Costs 
      (ii) 
      For Sell transactions: 
      MTA = [Unit x Selling Price] - Transaction Costs
    </language1>
    <language2>
      [Page: 7]
      39. Faedah Bonus Dagangan Saham atas baki kredit yang tersedia di dalam akan dikira secara 
      berperingkat berdasarkan jumlah amaun dagangan oleh Pemegang Akaun dalam bulan kalendar 
      (“Jumlah Amaun Dagangan Bulanan” atau “MTA”).

      Nota: 
      MTA dikira seperti berikut:

      (i) 
      Untuk transaksi Belian: 
      MTA = [Unit x Harga Belian] + Kos Transaksi 
      (ii) 
      Untuk transaksi Jualan: 
      MTA = [Unit x Harga Jualan] - Kos Transaksi
    </language2>
  </chunk>

      40. 
      The higher the MTA made in a month, the higher the Securities Trading Bonus Interest earned on 
      the credit balance in the Account.

      41. 
      The Securities Trading Bonus Interest is calculated based on the DAB of the Account as at the end of 
      each calendar month in accordance with the following formula:

      Securities Trading Bonus Interest = DAB x Time (T) x Rate (R)

      Note: 
      DAB 
      =    Sum of total end-day balance/Total number of days in the month 
      Time (T) 
      = Number of days in the month/Number of days in the year, based on a 365-day 
      calendar year 
      Rate (R) 
      = Securities Trading Bonus Interest rate based on the MTA as at month end
    </language1>
    <language2>
      [Page: 7]
      40. Semakin banyak MTA yang dibuat dalam sebulan, semakin tinggi Faedah Bonus Dagangan Saham 
      yang diperoleh atas baki kredit di dalam Akaun.

      41. Faedah Bonus Dagangan Saham dikira berdasarkan DAB Akaun pada akhir bulan setiap bulan seperti 
      formula di bawah:

      Faedah Bonus Dagangan Saham = DAB x Masa (T) x Kadar (R)

      Nota: 
      DAB 
      =   Jumlah baki akhir harian dalam bulan tersebut / JumlahBbilangan hari dalam 
      bulan tersebut

      Masa (T) 
      = Bilangan hari dalam bulan tersebut/ Bilangan hari dalam setahun, berdasarkan 
      365 hari setahun

      Kadar (R) 
      = Kadar Faedah Bonus Dagangan Saham berdasarkan MTA pada akhir bulan
    </language2>
  </chunk>
